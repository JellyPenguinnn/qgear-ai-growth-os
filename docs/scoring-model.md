# Scoring Model

The Q-GEAR score is a 100-point descriptive score.

| Component | Points |
|---|---:|
| AI infrastructure relevance | 12 |
| Business quality | 18 |
| Revenue and earnings acceleration | 18 |
| Earnings report / guidance / revisions | 17 |
| Valuation and expected IRR | 15 |
| Technical trend / relative strength | 10 |
| Portfolio fit / risk control | 10 |

The score alone never creates a buy or add action.

```text
Action = Score + Hard Gates + Risk Budget + Evidence Freshness
```

A lower price only improves valuation. It does not improve AI relevance, business quality, margins, execution, competitive advantage, or free cash flow.

## Calibration Note

The current v0.1 weights, linear cutoffs, 90-day evidence age, drawdown thresholds, and position caps are deterministic MVP heuristics. They are chosen to make the process explicit and testable. The research base supports the broad pillars, but it does not prove these exact weights or thresholds.
