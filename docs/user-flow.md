# User Flow

## v0.1 Flow

1. Configure onboarding settings: capital, currency, country, risk style, CAGR target, drawdown limit, cash buffer, max single-stock allocation, benchmarks, and manual portfolio mode.
2. Open the AI universe screener and filter by AI layer, score, state, growth, margin, and drawdown.
3. Open a stock deep dive to inspect business summary, AI thesis, evidence, metrics, valuation, technical state, portfolio risk, and decision state.
4. Approve a thesis by entering thesis statement, what must go right, what would break it, key metrics, and next review date.
5. User manually trades outside the app if desired.
6. Record manual positions.
7. Review concentration, drawdown mode, and benchmark placeholders.
8. Log every decision in the journal.
9. Review daily, weekly, monthly, quarterly, and annual report skeletons.

The daily brief should usually say no action unless evidence changed.

## Cadence

- Daily: light monitoring for thesis-changing evidence, stale evidence, technical damage, and earnings dates.
- Weekly: candidate ranking, valuation-zone changes, technical-wait changes, and cash/risk budget review.
- Monthly: portfolio concentration, drawdown, journal discipline, and benchmark placeholders.
- Quarterly: earnings checklist, thesis status changes, score changes, valuation updates, and action changes.
- Annual: benchmark audit versus SPY, QQQ, XLK, and SMH plus mistake review.
