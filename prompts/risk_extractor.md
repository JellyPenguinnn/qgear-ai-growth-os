# Risk Extractor Prompt

Extract risks that could invalidate or weaken the thesis.

Focus on:

- guidance cuts,
- margin deterioration,
- free cash flow weakness,
- balance-sheet risk,
- customer concentration,
- inventory/cycle risk,
- valuation/IRR failure,
- technical breakdown,
- portfolio concentration,
- stale evidence.

Return risks as evidence-backed objects with `claim`, `evidence`, `source`, `source_date`, `confidence`, and `disproves_if` fields.
